# Support

Thank you for your interest in **ideagraph**!

If you have questions, suggestions, or need help using the project, here are
some ways to get support:

## 💬 Ask a Question

If you're experiencing a problem or have a question about how to use
`ideagraph`, please open a
[GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/isaac-cf-wong/ideagraph/discussions) or
[Issue](https://github.com/isaac-cf-wong/ideagraph/issues), depending on the
nature of your question.

- Use **Discussions** for general questions, ideas, or usage help.
- Use **Issues** for reporting bugs or requesting new features.

## 🐛 Found a Bug

Please check if your issue has already been reported. If not, create a
[new issue](https://github.com/isaac-cf-wong/ideagraph/issues/new/choose) with:

- A clear and descriptive title.
- Steps to reproduce the problem.
- Relevant logs or error messages.
- The version of Python and `ideagraph` you're using.

## 🙋 Want to Contribute

Contributions are welcome! Please check out the
[Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md) and our
[Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to get started.

---

We appreciate your support and interest in the project!
